Who We Are

Scale Dynamics Limited LLC is a technology company at the intersection of faith and innovation. We believe that modern technology can make timeless Scripture more accessible, engaging, and meaningful for people around the world.

Our team of engineers, designers, and faith-minded creators built WePray with a simple vision: to help people develop a consistent, enriching Bible reading habit supported by the best tools technology has to offer.

From AI-powered Bible guidance to beautifully designed daily devotionals, every feature in WePray is crafted to serve your spiritual growth journey.

Scripture Meets Technology

Ask questions about any Bible passage, listen to Scripture on the go, track your spiritual growth, and build a daily habit of engaging with God's Word.

Our Mission

At Scale Dynamics Limited LLC, our mission is to make Bible study accessible, engaging, and transformative for everyone. We believe that understanding Scripture should not be limited by time, location, or resources.

We envision a world where anyone -- whether a lifelong believer or a curious newcomer -- can open their phone and immediately connect with the Word of God through reading, listening, asking questions, and tracking their spiritual journey.

Through continuous innovation and a deep respect for the sacred text, we strive to build the most helpful and thoughtful Bible companion available, contributing to spiritual growth and biblical literacy worldwide.

Features

📖

Bible Reading

Read the complete King James Version with intuitive book, chapter, and verse navigation. A clean, distraction-free reading experience designed for daily study.

✨

Daily Devotionals

Start each day with curated Scripture passages and thoughtful reflections. Build a consistent devotional habit that nourishes your faith throughout the week.

💬

AI Bible Guide

Ask questions about any passage, explore theological topics, and deepen your understanding of Scripture with our AI-powered Bible assistant, built on OpenAI technology.

Audio Listen

Listen to any Bible chapter read aloud using native iOS text-to-speech. Perfect for commutes, walks, or winding down before sleep.

Growth Tracking

Track your reading streaks, verses read, and devotional progress. Visualize your spiritual growth journey and stay motivated with daily milestones.

Privacy First

No account required. All your reading progress and preferences stay on your device. We believe your spiritual journey is personal and private.
