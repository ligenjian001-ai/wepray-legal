Privacy Policy

Scale Dynamics Limited LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the WePray: Daily Bible Verse mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Onboarding Preferences: Your faith journey stage, Bible reading frequency, and topic interests. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in the AI Chat feature are transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate responses. We do not store your chat history on our servers.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to WePray Pro, your payment is processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal contact information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features, including AI-powered Bible guidance

  • To generate AI-powered Bible study responses via OpenAI's API

  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts (install attribution)

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

We request your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before collecting your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). This identifier is used solely for install attribution analytics through Adjust. You can change your tracking preference at any time in:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → WePray

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your onboarding preferences, reading progress, and app settings are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data is not transmitted to any server.
  • Chat Data: Chat messages are processed by OpenAI's API in real-time and are not retained on our servers after the response is generated.
  • No Backend Servers: We do not operate our own backend servers that store user data. All server-side processing is handled by the third-party services listed above.
  • Security: We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect data transmitted to third-party services, including HTTPS encryption for all API communications.

6. Data Retention

  • Local app data is retained until you delete the App.
  • Analytics data is retained according to the retention policies of Firebase Analytics (default 14 months) and Adjust.
  • Chat messages are not retained after the AI response is generated.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about the data we collect about you.
  • Deletion: Delete the App to remove all locally stored data. For data held by third-party services, contact them directly using the links in Section 3.
  • Opt-Out of Tracking: Deny or revoke tracking permission via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Manage Subscriptions: Manage or cancel your subscription through Apple's subscription management (Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions).
  • Disable Analytics: You can limit analytics collection by disabling tracking in your device settings.

7.1 For California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

7.2 For European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights including the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your data, as well as the right to restrict and object to certain processing. Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (improving our service) and consent (for tracking). To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.
