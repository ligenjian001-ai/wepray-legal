Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

Scale Dynamics Limited LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the WePray: Daily Bible Verse mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Onboarding Preferences: Your faith journey stage, Bible reading frequency, and topic interests. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in the AI Chat feature are transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate responses. We do not store your chat history on our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as screen views, feature usage, and app performance metrics. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Attribution Data: We use Adjust SDK to understand how users discover our app. With your consent (via App Tracking Transparency), Adjust may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for attribution purposes.
  • Subscription Information: Purchase and subscription status is managed by Apple through StoreKit and is subject to Apple's privacy practices.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To personalize your devotional and Bible study experience
  • To generate AI-powered Bible guidance responses
  • To analyze app usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Processes chat messages to generate AI responses. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics (Google): Collects anonymized usage analytics. See Firebase Privacy Information.
  • Adjust: Provides install attribution. See Adjust's Privacy Policy.
  • Superwall: Manages paywall presentation. See Superwall's Privacy Policy.

4. App Tracking Transparency

We request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change this at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your onboarding preferences and reading progress are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. We do not operate backend servers that store your personal data. Chat messages are processed in real-time and are not retained after the response is generated.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny or revoke tracking permission via iOS Settings
  • Delete the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Manage your subscription through Apple's subscription management

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
